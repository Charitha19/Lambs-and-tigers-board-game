History

      Lambs and Tigers is a strategic, two-player board game 
      originated in Tamil Nadu(Aadu Puli Aattam) and is also 
      played in Karnataka(Huli gatta) and Andhra Pradesh
      (Puli Joodam or Puli - Meka). 
      The game is asymmetric in that one player controls three 
      tigers and the other player controls up to 15 goats. 
      The tigers 'hunt' the goats while the goats attempt to block 
      the tigers' movements. 
      In ancient days, we can see the engravings of the game 
      chart in every veranda.
